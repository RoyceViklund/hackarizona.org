Judging


Submit your hack on Sunday, 1/15 by 9AM on HackerEarth.


Steps to submit (one submission per team)

  1. 1) All team members register for HackerEarth
  2. 2) Create a team (one person per team does this) and invite all of your team members to your HackerEarth team once they've signed up.
  3. 3) Create your project submission (you may continue to make updates until 9AM) on HackerEarth by 9am.
  4. 4) Report to Grand Ballroom for table placement by 10am.
  5. 5) Demo their project during Project Exposition.

The Project Exposition will take place from 10:00am to 1:00pm Sunday morning inside the Student Union's Grand Ballroom. Sponsors, mentors, judges, and members of the public will be granted entry to view projects at 10:00am. Winners of Hack Arizona categories and sponsor prizes will be announced online by 11:59pm the following morning, Monday, January 16th.

Grand Ballroom Map



Judging Criteria

Originality: Has your project been done before? Is there novelty present?
Creativity: Does your project have a non-obvious key element? Is there artistic merit present?
Difficulty: How hard was your project to pull off during the event? Did you pull it off?
Value Proposition: Does the project demonstrate a clear utility and is the team able to communicate that?
