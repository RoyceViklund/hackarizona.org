Open Positions

  • Organizer

    Logistics Team

    Hosting one of the largest hackathons in the U.S. is not an easy feat logistically. The logistics team serves as the backbone of Hack Arizona’s flagship hackathon and other events programs. As a member of the Logistics Team you have the opportunity to work on improving the experience of Hack Arizona’s hackathon and other programs by building the logistical framework for new and current programs and iniatives.

    Responsibilities

    • Scheduling of programs
    • Venue planning and management
    • Resource management
    • Volunteer coordination

    Qualifications

    • You have great Attention to detail
    • You have strong communication skills
    • You are excited about building great event experiences
    • Past even planning experience is a plus, but not required.

  • Organizer

    Sponsorship Team

    Hack Arizona’s sponsors and partners are the stakeholders which make our flagship hackathon and many of our other programs possible. As a member of the Sponsorship Team you have the opportunity to interact and engage with some of the world’s leading technology companies and organizations that power Hack Arizona’s current and future programs.

    Responsibilities

    • Engaging with new sponsors and partners
    • Maintaining present sponsor and partner relationships
    • Sponsor fundraising
    • Financial processes around sponsorship

    Qualifications

    • You are mission-driven
    • You want talk about Hack Arizona’s mission and the value we provide to external organizations
    • You are excited about talking with companies and organization of all shapes and sizes
    • Past sales or customer experience roles is a plus, but not required.

  • Organizer

    Marketing Team

    Getting the word out about Hack Arizona’s hackathon and other programs is a vital component to Hack Arizona’s success and longevity. Hack Arizona serves students and the channels to access students spans a broad and complex array of mediums and platforms. As a member of the Marketing Team you have the opportunity to help attract Hack Arizona’s diehard fans to our programs and events and as well as engage with demographics unfamiliar with Hack Arizona.

    Responsibilities

    • Content curation
    • Email campaigns
    • Social media strategy
    • Event media production

    Qualifications

    • You are excited about creating social content that our audience loves
    • You are familiar with social media tools and platforms
    • You can communicate Hack Arizona’s programs and their value to our audience
    • Past social media or marketing roles is a plus, but not required.

  • Organizer

    Finance Team

    Behind the scenes of Hack Arizona’s hackathon and other programs are the financial processes that fuel everything from purchasing materials and resources for our programs to our own internal tools, budgeting, sponsorship pipeline and University of Arizona account management. As a member of the Finance Team you are at the forefront of big decisions being made, the creation of new iniatives and the processes that allow us to be more efficient and effective with our resources.

    Responsibilities

    • Setting and allocating budgets for current and new iniatives
    • Allocating resources for new iniatives
    • Managing sponsor contributions
    • Purchasing, accounting and reimbursement processes

    Qualifications

    • You get excited about good processes and managing potentially large funds
    • You have strong communication skills
    • You want to participate in making financial decisions, even challenging the challenging decisions
    • Past accounting or finance roles are a plus, but not required.
